Server Rules

Keep the forge clean. Break these rules and face the ban hammer.

01

No Cheating or Hacking

Use of hacked clients, x-ray, auto-clickers, or any unfair advantage is strictly prohibited. We have advanced anti-cheat.

02

Be Respectful

No racism, hate speech, or excessive toxicity in chat. We want a competitive but welcoming environment.

03

No Bug Abuse

If you find a glitch or bug, report it on Discord. Abusing it for gain will result in a wipe or ban.

04

No Teaming in Solos

In Free-For-All modes, teaming is not allowed. Please play the game as intended.
